How Formula 1 Cars Work

Learn about the technology behind the fastest racing cars in the world.

F1 Engine

Formula 1 cars use powerful 1.6-litre V6 turbo hybrid engines. These engines combine fuel power with electrical energy recovery systems to produce over 1000 horsepower. The hybrid technology helps improve efficiency while maintaining incredible speed on the race track.

Aerodynamics

Aerodynamics is one of the most important parts of an F1 car. Wings, floor designs, and bodywork help create downforce, pushing the car onto the track. More downforce allows drivers to corner at higher speeds while maintaining grip and stability.
