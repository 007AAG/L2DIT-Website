What is Formula 1 Racing

The world's fastest and most exciting racing series

What is Formula 1?

Formula 1 is the highest level of international motorsport. It features the fastest racing cars, the best drivers, and races held all around the world. Millions of fans watch every season as teams compete for the Constructors' Championship and drivers fight for the World Championship.

Fastest Cars

Modern Formula 1 cars can exceed 350 km/h.

Global Sport

Races take place across Europe, Asia, America, and the Middle East.

World Champions

Legendary drivers include Ayrton Senna, Michael Schumacher, and Lewis Hamilton.

Some of the Legends

Some of the most historic Formula 1 drivers such as Ayrton Senna, Michael Schumacher, and Alain Prost.

How F1 Cars are Different From Regular Cars

Formula 1 cars are lighter, faster, and more aerodynamic than regular road cars.

Why Formula 1 is a Global Sport

Formula 1 races take place across multiple continents and attract millions of fans worldwide.
