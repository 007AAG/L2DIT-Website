Front Wing

The front wing is located at the front of the car. It controls airflow around the vehicle and creates downforce, helping the car grip the track when cornering.

Rear Wing

The rear wing produces a large amount of downforce. It helps keep the rear of the car stable and improves cornering performance at high speeds.

Suspension

The suspension connects the wheels to the chassis. It helps absorb bumps, maintain tyre contact with the track, and improves handling and stability.
